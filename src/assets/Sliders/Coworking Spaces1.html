We Offer Creative Working Environments That Suit Your Business

CS CO WORKING SPACES was founded in the year 2018. With constantly increasing prices in the real estate for commercial spaces we came up with the idea of co working spaces at very affordable and reliable prices. We are operating in 4 different locations across Hyderabad namely Kondapur, Shaikpet, Gachibowli and HITEC-city with over 100 plus clients as our customers.
